About Us

Welcome to our website for Smokey’s Rust Console Roleplay Server! Our owner, Smokey, is a passionate gamer dedicated to creating a chill and inviting space for all players. With the help of his friends and admins, Smokey ensures that our server is well-moderated and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you’re a seasoned player or new to the game, you’ll find a friendly and supportive community here. Join us and be part of the adventure!

Our team is committed to fostering a positive and engaging environment where players can immerse themselves in the world of Rust. We host regular events, offer helpful resources, and are always open to feedback to improve your experience. Come and explore, build, and survive with us in a community that values respect, creativity, and fun!
